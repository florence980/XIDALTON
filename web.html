About Us

Welcome to 11-Dalton STEM at San Jose Litex Senior High School! We are a group of students passionate about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our goal is to explore these subjects through hands-on learning and teamwork, preparing ourselves for future challenges. We strive for academic excellence while working together on projects that can make a positive difference in our school and community. This website helps us share our journey, inspiring both ourselves and others along the way.
